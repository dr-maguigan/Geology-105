How does this class work?

To begin, the syllabus is HERE. Each lesson will begin with the expectations (what will be covered? questions to answer, etc.). Throughout the lesson, there will be stops for recaps and checks for understanding (RCUs). Questions are welcome at any time during the lesson and they are appreciated. Each lesson will end by recapping the whole lesson by going over what was covered or the questions to be answered. If you have questions on any of these points, please feel free to ask in class or in an email.

How will I be assessed?

Always the main question at the beginning of the course. This might be where things depart from other classes.
I will be using a competency-based approach, which asks the question "Can you do it or not?" That means you get either 100% or 0% of the points for that assessment.
To earn the competency, you will need a passing grade of 70% or higher (when applicable). For these, you will get 2 attempts.
Some assessments will have you complete a set of questions with a code to enter on Moodle when you arrive at the correct ending.
There will be 24 competency assessments (all on Moodle or on this site) over the semester plus a final exam (6 points) on Moodle. Most are worth 1 point.
Deadlines are posted with the assessment on Moodle.
There are 30 points possible, including the final. The breakdown is as follows:

  • 27+ points = A
  • 24-26 = B
  • 20-23 = C
  • 17-19 = D
    • <17 = F
